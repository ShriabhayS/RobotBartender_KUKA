Robotic Bartender

We built something to make ordinary moments a little magical.

We learned by doing. Small, safe motion steps, better sensors, and clear UI made the robot more reliable. This project picks a bottle and delivers it to you — opened or closed — and celebrates curiosity, iteration and careful engineering.

How it works

  • Simple UI for choosing a drink and whether you want it opened.
  • Reliable control that breaks tasks into safe primitives for the KUKA arm.
  • Sensors & opener that detect bottle presence and open when requested.

Make sure the robot's workspace is clear, and enjoy the show — but always supervise hardware while it runs.
